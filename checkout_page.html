WHERE’S MY CREDIT?

Schoola offers credits to help spread the word that there is a new way for people to support their schools. The more who know about us, the more schools we can help. Everybody wins.

To continue doing this, we need to make sure that schools are actually being helped. To prevent credits from being abused, we run some checks. If those checks come back a certain way, we will not allow the credit to be used.

We’re making the system smarter all the time, and we thank you for your understanding and your focus on helping schools.

    Shipping

    How long before my order is shipped?

    Orders will ship lickety-split! They will be on their way to you in 1-3 business days from the purchase date.

    What do you charge for shipping?

    Shipping for orders totaling $50 or more (before tax) are free! Shipping starts at $4.99 for your first item, and costs a mere 99¢ for each additional item. Your loot will usually arrive within 3-7 business days, so shop to your little heart’s content! We can handle it.

    What are your shipping options?

    To keep costs low and give as much as possible to schools, we currently only offer standard shipping via FedEx SmartPost. We are unable to split your order between multiple destinations. Please contact us if you have further shipping questions.

    Will you ship my order anywhere in the world?

    Currently, we can only ship orders to the 48 contiguous United States and Hawaii. We cannot fulfill orders for Alaska, Guam, Puerto Rico, The U.S. Virgin Islands, American Samoa, or outside the U.S. Check back with us from time to time, because we’re working on expanding our shipping locations!

    What is your return policy?

    Our goal is to give back as much as we can to our participating schools and one way we are able to do that at this point is to not offer returns. We inspect and price items accordingly.If you are unsatisfied with your purchase, we will always try to make it right.

    Why can't you do returns?

    Our goal is to give back as much as we can to our participating schools and one way we are able to do that at this point is to not offer returns. We inspect and price items accordingly.If you are unsatisfied with your purchase, we will always try to make it right.
